# Story 5.9: Privacy Policy and Terms Pages

## Status

Approved

## Story

**As a** user,  
**I want** to view the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service,  
**so that** I understand how my data is handled and the usage terms.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Privacy Policy page accessible from footer and settings
2. Terms of Service page accessible from footer and settings
3. Pages are readable without authentication
4. Pages load instantly (static content, no API calls)
5. Privacy Policy explains: data collected, storage, sharing, retention, deletion rights
6. Terms of Service explains: acceptable use, disclaimers, liability limitations
7. Last updated date displayed on each page
8. Mobile-responsive layout
